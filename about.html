About Us

A Few Words About Us

We are not an educational institution, we are social initiative.

Our goal is to help graduate orphans and underprivileged children to get knowledge and skills in computer science and programming, to become society’s leaders, become strong Christians, get employed and help next generation in their turn. We provide full boarding: accomodation, food, medicine, stipend, and, most importantly, modern education program. Why we have chosen IT sphere: because IT is one of the most, if not the most, dynamically developing professions in Ukraine. Our graduates should not be left unemployed and, therefore, at the low social level.

Timeline

In January have been formed solid concept and exact plans regarding non-profit Hebron and for-profit Hebron Cluster. Active work to create social entrepreneurship, called Hebron cluster has been done. It is for-profit IT company to target the social goals. Most part of Hebron’s students practise their theoretical knowledge and get practical skills, progressing on market twice faster, therefore, get socially rehabilitated.

In February the idea of creating social initiative was brought to create educational project for orphans and underprivileged children in need. In August, IT Academy Hebron was registered In September, education process started. Students have been effectively taking education plan step by step in hope of better future.

In 950 B.C., in the south of Judea (now territory of Israel), David founded the city Hebron. All that time, goodness and welfare was present there. People got very well along, they were good salepeople and shared among themselves. People did not fight, but loved God. It was a blessed city!
