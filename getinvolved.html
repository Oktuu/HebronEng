Actually, it's very easy to help Hebron Academy. Even a small contribution already matters for our social education organisation. Paying attention to our process gives us courage and passion to change lives for better fo those who need it; to go forward step-by-step and create a future of Ukraine. Hebron is living only by support of volunteering contributions.

There are different ways to support Hebron:

- Sponsoring one of our Students

- Giving Money Contributions

- Contributing used laptop/education materials/books

- Becoming a volunteer in our working environment

Every way of support is much appreciated. From our side, we give all the attention and thanks we can to our supporters

HEBRON

Education Charity Organization

Mission

To form society’s leaders based on Christian values. These leaders will possess high quality skills in High Tech profession. These young people come from underprivileged social circles

Vision

To be the center of innovative education among orphans and underprivileged youth. It will be combined with Biblical upbringing, online education and new resources

Help to one student

Company, organization, family can take student for personal upkeep. In this way student will be provided with everything needed for living and studying in Hebron. You will also support our organization. We are lacking personal support for just 3 students. For now the upkeep for one student is 300 $. This include : 50 $, which is financial aid to student (money for personal needs, this sum is given to student), 50 $ for food, 200 $, which goes to accommodation upkeep, medical assurance, salaries to teachers and Hebron’s staff, literature for studying, household chemistry and so on. If you decide to take one of the boys for personal support, you will be able to maintain permanent contact with him. This will allow you to be sure, that your donation wisely used and that you are good mentor for student.

Mentoring and Volunteering

We need volunteering aid, especially from people well-educated in sphere of programming and IT technologies. Now we are in search of experienced front-end mentors, people, who will conduct workshops in programming and personal mentors, who will help students with IT studies. Also we need mentors, who will conduct different lectures on topics of leadership, christian upbringing, bad habits, personal and professional development. Also we need people, who could conduct studying courses on matter of certain theme or subject for our students, on volunteering basis or partial paying. Teachers on such subjects are needed: programming, English and Ukrainian language, history of Ukraine, leadership and general development. We will also be glad to see people, who could help us with household activities and cooking. For additional information about mentoring and volunteering contact Ihor Bula (+38(098) 119-17-61)

Providing Laptops

We will be glad to get any used devices, especially used notebooks. At this moment organization can’t provide every student with computers. Almost all of our computers are dated and don’t work properly. It’s important to us having additional computers, which can be send throughout entire Ukraine to potential students, who can not afford themselves to have own computers. Computers will be filled with materials on English education, IT technologies and general development. Also it will have access to free online courses platforms. In this way we will be helping children outside Hebron too, rise their academical level, mentor them, be in touch with them and prepare them for entering into our facility. If there will be chance, we would like to get devices, especially notebooks that will work properly.
