Volodymyr Raguz

Siversk, orphan

I was born in 1999 in Belhorod oblast in Russia. My mother was Ukrainian and my father - Russian. When I was 3 when my mother died and my father got into prison. My little brother (2 y.o) and I were practically alone, but our grandparents knew about this and took us to Ukraine, Siversk city in Donetsk oblast were I lived until 12. After that we were taken to Slovjanskiy centre of rehabilitation, and my brother and me were sent to different internats, he was sent to Mykolaivka, me - to Marjinka. I finished 9 years of school there. After some time I got news that my grandparents died and my father, too. July 11th of 2014 our town was bombed and all the children were sent to Sviatohirsk, we had a hope to come back but unfortunately there was literally no town to come back to anymore. After finishing my school (11 years) I entered Donbas National Aotumotive Academy in Kramatorsk city, studied for one year but then got to know about Hebron and immidiately realized I want to come here. Right after I hit my 18 years old, I came to Hebron and I'm happy for that.
