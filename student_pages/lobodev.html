Aleksey Lobodev

Khmelnitsky,an orphan

My name is Aleksey Lobodiev, born in June 1999. I'm originally from Khmelnitsky oblast, Ukraine. I live with my older sister Olga. Our father does not contact with us at all. Our mother was murdered in an attack by two men in 2015. My family is very poor. I attended church since childhood. I studied at Khmelnytsky school №20 through grade 9. After high school, I studied at Khmelnitsky Professional Lyceum of Electronics. My hobbies are: music and singing, drawing (when I'm in the mood), astronomy (I love space), and technology (phones, headphones, etc.). I studied programming since February 2016; in the class "Fundamentals of Programming", I received the highest mark. I wanted to study software engineering, but it was not possible financially for me. My friends at church told me about Hebron Academy, so I decided to go to Hebron to gain knowledge and to have the profession that I dream about.
