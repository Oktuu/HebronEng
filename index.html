Social project for orphans and unprivileged boys

In Ukraine there is a big question what to do once an orphan turns 18, graduates and enters into the system. Usually these children turn out to be not adapted to independent life. They enter low-level colleges, and without life motivation they do not pay proper attention to education. They ineffectively waste their time and efforts. At some point they come across obstacles.

Our Process

HOW ACADEMY WORKS

PRE-SELECTION

Every year we are picking candidates for education, if they expressed wish to study IT. Those candidates are orphans, or they are from underprivileged families(social category).

TESTING PERIOD

We are trying to figure out, whether candidate can cope with educational program, if he agrees to set of rules, which he must stick to, while he studying at Hebron, whether he is ready to work on himself, which is actually hard work. Every student, who passed testing period is provided with accommodation, nutrition, money support and high-quality education.

STUDY PERIOD

Education lasts for 2 years. Students study different subjects: Christian ethics, computer programming, web-design, critical thinking, math and English.

GETTING EMPLOYED

Our aim is to help our students get a job in different IT companies after they graduate. This is not our duty, we are making it on our will. Still, it depends on the level of personal knowledge of person, who graduates, and his own traits.
